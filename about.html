Our Story

"Born from passion, served with elegance."

Bon Appétit was founded in 2020 with a simple vision: to blend the rich, traditional flavors of Bangladesh with the finesse of global haute cuisine. Our journey began in a small kitchen in Chattogram, driven by a passion for authentic ingredients and culinary innovation.

Today, we are proud to offer a dining experience that is both luxurious and comforting, elegant yet welcoming. Each dish is a work of art, crafted by chefs who believe that food is a celebration of life.
